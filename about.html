Get To Know William

Hi, I am William,

I was born & raised in Norfolk, Virginia but I've had the privilege of living all over the United States as member of the United States Army, I currently call Natick, MA home. I'm fueled by boundless enthusiasm & hope, I’m easily inspired and willing to follow my fascinations wherever they take me.

I’m passionate, expressive, and posse a habit for finding the silver lining in every situation. I’m never satisfied to just complete a task or a project. Instead I have an almost impulsive need to perfect it. I’m a people-person with deep emotions and empathy, I consider myself a natural storyteller.
